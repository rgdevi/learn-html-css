Sambaar Rice

This is one of the favourite foods, right combination of protein from daal and carb from rice along with all the vegetables with the right mix of sambaar masala with some ghee!

Ingredients

  • Rice -1 cup
  • Toor daal 1/2 cup
  • Onion 1/2
  • Tomato 2
  • Beans 10
  • Carrot 1
  • Sambaar Powder - 2 Spoons
  • Turmeric Powder-1/4 of a spoon
  • Salt -to taste
  • Asafetida-1/4 of a spoon
  • Curry Leaves-10 leaves
  • Clinatro-few bunch
  • Dry Red Chilly-2 nos
  • Oil-2TBsp
  • Ghee-1TBsp
  • Vessel to Cook -Indian Pressure Cooker/Slow cooker
  • Water - 5 cups
  • Refer Optional Section as needed

Method of Preparation

  1. Mix both Rice and Daal and soak for 30 mins with 4.5 cups of water
  2. Cut all veggies mentioned in Ingredients
  3. Take cooker, add oil and let it warm up
  4. Add Mustard and let it splutter
  5. Then add urad daal, red chilly, asafetida, curry leaf
  6. First satuatte onion then followed by other veggies and bring to half cooked
  7. Now add Sambaar powder 2 spoon, turmeric , salt and mix all of them together
  8. Now add mix of rice and daal with that water
  9. Wait for1 whistle and keep it off the stove
  10. Open the vessel when the pressure is fully released and add choppped cilantro
  11. Add one spoon ghee mix with rice and ready to serve

Optional:

  1. You can boil one potato, mash and add to sambaar rice, this will enhance the taste
  2. Heat coriander seeds, pepper and then grind it and add it along with this to get good aroma
